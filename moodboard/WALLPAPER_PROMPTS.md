## Style modifiers (append to any prompt)

- **Pixel art**: `, 16-bit pixel art style, clean pixel grid, no anti-aliasing, retro game aesthetic`
- **Painterly**: `, digital painting, atmospheric, concept art quality, artstation`
- **Photorealistic**: `, photorealistic, 8K, volumetric lighting, depth of field, Unreal Engine`
- **Dark/moody boost**: `, extremely dark and oppressive, minimal lighting, heavy vignette, film grain`

## Key mood words to mix in

`oppressive` `concrete` `smog-choked` `authoritarian` `institutional` `surveillance` `crumbling` `brutalist` `prefab` `panelka` `dystopian` `harsh lighting` `desaturated`

## Anti-words (never include)

`bright` `cheerful` `colorful` `fantasy` `magical` `sunny` `vibrant` `lush` `green` `peaceful`
